About me

Hello! I'm Nahunda Bekele, a versatile creative professional based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. With a passion for technology and storytelling, I specialize in web development, digital marketing, graphic design, and video editing.

My journey began with web development, where I honed my skills in building responsive and user-friendly websites. As I delved deeper, I discovered the power of digital marketing to connect brands with their audiences, leading me to develop strategies that drive engagement and growth.

Simultaneously, my interest in visual storytelling led me to graphic design and video editing. I enjoy crafting compelling visuals and narratives that resonate with viewers, whether it's through striking designs or engaging video content.

I believe in continuous learning and adaptability, always eager to embrace new challenges and technologies. Collaboration is at the heart of my work, and I thrive in environments where ideas are shared, and creativity is nurtured.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to see examples of my work across these disciplines. If you're interested in collaborating or have any questions, don't hesitate to reach out!
